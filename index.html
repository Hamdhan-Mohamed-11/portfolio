About

Professional Background and Specialization

Malcolm Lismore is a dedicated freelance photographer based on the North West coast of Scotland, with over a decade of experience and a profound passion for capturing the natural world. Specializing in landscape, wildlife, and coastal bird photography, Malcolm's work showcases the rugged beauty of the Scottish landscape, reflecting a deep connection to nature and bringing out the unique charm and majesty of Scotland's mountains, lochs, and wildlife.

Awards and Recognition

Malcolm's photography has been featured in numerous exhibitions across Scotland and the UK, earning him accolades such as the Scottish Nature Photographer of the Year and the Highland Wildlife Award. His striking images have been published in various nature magazines, including "Scottish Wildlife" and "Nature's Best Photography."

Event and Portrait Photography

In addition to his nature photography, Malcolm is available for weddings, portraits, and special events. Known for his attention to detail and creative approach, he excels at capturing the essence of special moments, making him a sought-after photographer for a variety of occasions. His wedding photography has been celebrated for its ability to capture the joy and emotion of the day, while his portrait work is renowned for its depth and character.

Workshops and Tours

Malcolm regularly conducts photography workshops and tours, sharing his expertise and passion with aspiring photographers. His workshops, which range from beginner to advanced levels, are highly regarded for their hands-on approach and personalized instruction. Whether capturing the majesty of Scotland's landscapes or the intimate moments of a special event, Malcolm Lismore's photography continues to inspire and captivate audiences, making him one of Scotland's premier photographers.

Portrait Photography

Event Photography
